Thaparlympics

How's the Josh?

Thaparlympics is one of the premier sports events in the North and not only thaparians but outsiders too agree to that. Therefore, like any other major event, we felt the need to bring Thaparlympics at the ease of Internet. The main purpose of this project is to simplify the process of handling all the sports events organized during Thaparlympics by providing a web interface for organizers and participants.

The admin has the power of manipulating events by adding the where’s and when’s of the event, and the control of allotting faculty and students to organising committee. Willing participants have the facility to register for any sports activity. Lastly, viewing the results of the events is also available. Hence, this sums up our little efforts to honour every individual associated with Thaparlympics.
